Pablo and Codex reframed Itera's product hypothesis after identifying the underlying problem: the course felt too theoretical, and the benefit for companies was hard to make tangible.

**New direction:** Itera should not position itself as "another AI course," an automation tool like n8n/Make/Zapier, or a wrapper around ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini. The most defensible space is:

> Itera is an enterprise simulator for practicing AI judgment in realistic workplace situations.

**What this means:**
- The primary unit should move from only "lesson" toward "simulation."
- Evidence for companies does not need to be immediate hard ROI or a production automation workflow.
- Evidence should show readiness: judgment, instruction quality, response evaluation, risk handling, privacy awareness, tool choice, and ability to apply AI with reasonable supervision.
- Itera remains education. It does not execute work for the company; it trains employees to use existing tools better.

**Do not compete against:**
- ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Codex, Veo, Nano Banana, or other generative engines.
- n8n, Zapier, Make, CRMs, chatbots, or automation platforms.
- Generic LMS platforms whose main value is catalog, video, paths, and certificates.

**Target intersection:**

```
AI tools -> workplace situation -> guided practice -> judgment -> evidence for company
```

**Comparables to research before implementation:**
- Wharton Interactive: business simulations for applying judgment in safe environments.
- Forage: job simulations with tasks that replicate real work.
- Attensi / Mursion / Whatfix Mirror: simulation, roleplay, readiness, and measurable performance.
- Section AI / DataCamp / Coursera / Udemy Business: AI adoption and upskilling for companies.
- Platzi Business / Crehana / Coderhouse: LATAM corporate training and local gaps.

**Why this matters:** the market already has access to AI. The company problem is not access or information; it is developing distributed judgment to use AI safely, productively, and measurably at work.

**Status:** strategic direction agreed for research. This does not imply building features yet. Before pivoting product/copy/landing/onboarding, run category and competitor benchmarking.

**Operating phrase:** "Itera is a flight simulator for teams to practice real AI decisions before applying them in real work."
