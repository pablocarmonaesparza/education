# Customer Advisory Board v2 — diseño

## TL;DR

Customer Advisory Board (CAB) es un grupo de 4-6 customers seleccionados que se reúnen trimestralmente para feedback estratégico sobre Itera. NO es focus group ni un sales mechanism — es relationship-building con power users + signal pipeline para decisions críticas.

**Trigger formar:** ≥5 customers v1 cerrados + ≥2 con repeat contract (RCR > 0). Estimado timing: F3 v2 roadmap (~6 meses post-customer-zero).

**NO formar:** pre-customer-zero. CAB sin customers es theater.

## Por qué CAB importa (cuando llegue)

Beyond feedback_loop_methodology bi-weekly retros, CAB ofrece:

1. **Signal estratégico cross-customer** — patterns que individual customers no ven
2. **Validation pre-commit** de roadmap items grandes (carrera expansion, pricing changes)
3. **Brand champions ecosystem** — CAB members likely become advocates/referrers
4. **Trust signal a prospects** — "tenemos CAB con [X] orgs" es credibilidad B2B
5. **Pricing/positioning testing** — CAB ve cambios primero, signal honesto

**ROI esperado:** ~30% de CAB members become customer-zero referrers (Bessemer 2024 SoC).

## Criterios de invitación

### Hard requirements (must-have)

1. **Active customer** ≥1 sprint cerrado + paid invoice
2. **Manager-level decision-maker** (Head/VP Marketing/Growth/Ops — M9-3-D14)
3. **LATAM mid-market** (50-300 empleados, MX/CO/AR/CL/BR)
4. **NPS post-sprint ≥7** (filter detractors — they need different channel)
5. **Quality bar:** rated reporte como útil en exit survey (FSRR ≥ "Quizás")

### Soft preferences (nice-to-have)

1. **Industry diversity:** mix SaaS B2B + ecommerce + servicios profesionales
2. **Tier diversity:** mix Diagnóstico + Sprint + Track customers
3. **Geo diversity:** ≥2 países (no todos MX si v1 cross-LATAM)
4. **Tenure diversity:** mix recently cerrado + customer ≥3 meses (signals evolución)
5. **LinkedIn presence:** active LinkedIn ≥5K followers (potential amplification)

### Exclusions

- ❌ Customers en active dispute (Categorías 3/6 dispute_resolution)
- ❌ Customers con quality dispute pendiente (hybrid review activa)
- ❌ Customers que pidieron full refund extra-policy en últimos 6 meses
- ❌ Customers reps de competidor org (conflict of interest)

## Formato operacional

### Tamaño

**4-6 members optimal.**

- <4: signal limitado, cancellation 1 person = drop a 3
- 7+: meetings unwieldy + harder to schedule

### Cadencia

**Trimestral** (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4). 4 meetings/año.

Alineado con competitive_pulse cadence (M9-3-D9) para Pablo dual review.

### Duración

**90 minutos** por meeting. Más es fatigoso. Menos no permite depth.

Format:
- 10 min: welcome + agenda recap (Pablo)
- 20 min: producto update (lanzamientos, métricas aggregate sin PII)
- 40 min: deep dive de 1 topic estratégico (rotating: pricing / roadmap / positioning / etc.)
- 15 min: open floor para CAB members raise issues
- 5 min: closing + next steps

### Channel

Q1: in-person CDMX o BOG si geográficamente viable (Pablo flies + dinner post-meeting).

Q2+: video call (Zoom/Google Meet). In-person 1x/año max.

### Compensation

NO cash payment.

Compensation explícita:
- **Early access** a features nuevas (matriz 3×5 cuando entre B5-002, etc.)
- **Discount future contracts:** 15% off Sprint o Track renewal
- **Co-marketing opportunities:** case study spotlight (con consent M9-3-D47)
- **Direct line to Pablo:** WhatsApp/Signal direct (response <24h)
- **CAB-only Slack channel** (or equivalent) para peer networking entre members

**Total cost:** $0 cash + ~12 horas/año Pablo time (4 meetings × 90 min + prep).

## Agenda topics por meeting

### Q1 (kick-off CAB) — ~6 meses post-customer-zero

**Topic:** ¿Qué está rompiendo Itera para tu org?

- Each member shares: 1 thing working + 1 thing breaking
- Pablo categorizes feedback A-G (feedback_loop_methodology)
- Output: top 3 actionable improvements + 1 strategic question for Q2

### Q2 — ~9 meses post-customer-zero

**Topic:** Carrera expansion priorities

- Pablo presents M9-3-D26 roadmap (Sales → CS → Ops)
- CAB votes priority (rank order ✓) + razones
- Discussion: ¿alguna carrera missing? ¿algún caso missing?
- Output: confirm v2 roadmap o pivot

### Q3 — ~12 meses post-customer-zero

**Topic:** Pricing + tier structure

- Pablo presents current pricing + M9-3-D6 upgrade evaluation
- CAB feedback: "esperaba pagar más" signal? "Caro para mi tamaño"?
- Discussion: tier structure refinement (lite tier? enterprise tier?)
- Output: pricing decision con counsel of CAB

### Q4 — ~15 meses post-customer-zero

**Topic:** Annual retro + v3 roadmap

- Aggregate métricas year-1 (con CAB consent on display)
- CAB shares stories case study material
- Discussion: ¿qué hicimos bien? ¿qué pivote requires?
- Output: v3 strategic direction + CAB renewal decisions

**Acceptance rate target:** ~50-70% de invitations. Si <50%, soft signal de CAB not valued — re-evaluate concept.

## Cuándo NO formar CAB

Anti-patterns que invalidan CAB v1:

### ❌ Anti-pattern 1: formar pre-customer-zero

CAB sin customers = focus group de prospects. Distinct mechanism. NO CAB hasta ≥5 customers paid.

### ❌ Anti-pattern 2: incluir prospects

CAB es customer-only. Prospect feedback va a discovery calls.

### ❌ Anti-pattern 3: cash compensation

Pay-to-attend rompe relationship integrity. Compensation via early access + discount + co-marketing es valid; cash es transaccional.

### ❌ Anti-pattern 4: focus group framing

"Vamos a testear el copy del email" es focus group, NO CAB. CAB es strategic-level (pricing, roadmap, positioning), no tactical.

### ❌ Anti-pattern 5: CAB > 8 members

Beyond 8, dynamics se vuelven manageable pero diluted. Mejor 4-6 active.

### ❌ Anti-pattern 6: skip meetings cuando "no hay nothing to discuss"

Cadence trimestral fixed. Si no hay topic crítico, focus en relationship-building + customer storytelling. NO skip — rompe rhythm.

## Outputs esperados year-1

Asumiendo CAB activates en F3 v2 roadmap (~6 meses post-customer-zero):

| Outcome | Probability | Value |
|---|---|---|
| ≥2 case studies con consent | 80% | Co-marketing material premium |
| ≥3 referrals to other prospects | 60% | Customer-zero acquisition channel |
| ≥1 testimonial video | 40% | Premium social proof |
| ≥1 LinkedIn recommendation | 70% | Pablo personal brand boost |
| Strategic pivot decisión informada | 90% | High value if pivot needed |
| Pricing decisión informada | 90% | High value pre-tier change |

**ROI:** $0 cash cost + ~12h/año Pablo = ~$2-4K equivalent. Outputs valued ~$10-30K equivalent. 5-10x ROI estimated.
